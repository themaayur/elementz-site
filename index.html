App Features

Some of the key features of the app, that everyone has appreciated.

Wikipedia intergration

Wikipedia button for getting extra knowledge about the element.

Covered Entire Periodic Table

Information about all the 118 Elements of periodic table.

Minimal & Simple UI

UI of the app is very minimal and not harsh to eyes with soft colours and simple interface.

Verified Information

All the information is verified form responsible sources.

Elementz gives information of 20+ properties regarding the element.
